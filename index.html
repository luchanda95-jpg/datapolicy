How Direct Access App keeps your data safe

This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We aim to keep things clear and simple.

1) Data we may collect

Direct Access App collects only what is needed to provide the service. Depending on features you use, we may collect:

  • Account details: name, email, phone number (if you create an account).
  • App usage data: basic analytics like screens visited, feature usage, and error logs to improve performance.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, app version (for troubleshooting and compatibility).
  • Content you provide: forms you submit, messages, or uploads (only if the app has these features).
  • Location (optional): only if you enable a feature that needs it, and you can turn it off anytime.

2) How we use your data

  • To provide core app features (sign-in, saving your info, showing your records).
  • To improve the app (fix bugs, reduce crashes, make features faster).
  • To communicate with you (support requests, important service updates).
  • To prevent fraud and keep users safe (security monitoring and abuse prevention).

3) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when necessary, such as:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) to run and improve the app.
  • Legal reasons if required by law or to protect user safety.
  • With your consent when you choose to connect to a third-party service.

4) Security measures

We use practical security controls to protect your data, including:

  • Access control (only authorized staff can access systems when needed).
  • Secure authentication (password rules and session controls where applicable).
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for data sent between the app and server.
  • Regular updates and monitoring to reduce vulnerabilities.

5) Data retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for the app to work and to meet legal or operational requirements. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

6) Your choices & controls

  • You can update or correct your account information in the app (if available).
  • You can disable permissions (like location) in your phone settings.
  • You can request export or deletion of your data by contacting us.
